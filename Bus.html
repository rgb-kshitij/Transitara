Live Bus Tracking

Travellers can track their bus in real-time using a bus tracking tool, guaranteeing that travellers don't miss their bus. When you're going to a new destination or when you need to board the bus at the right location, this live bus tracking feature is quite helpful.

Free Cancellation of Bus Tickets Policy

Transitara understands that last-minute changes can be challenging, and that's why we offer flexible cancellation policies. On Transitara platform, it's simple to cancel your bus reservation, get the instant refund into the wallet and you can re-book your bus ticket instantly without having to wait for the payment process.

Transitara offers free cancellations on all bus tickets starting at Rs.9 per seat. You can choose to cancel your trip for free and get a refund if you change your travel plans. Either the Transitara website or the Mobile App can be used to submit a ticket cancellation request.

Online Bus Booking in India

It's easy, simple and affordable to book your bus tickets online in India when you use the Transitara portal. Travellers can select their bus route and check schedules, choose their favorite bus seat, then pay using a number of payment online methods and avail discounts. Transitara will send a booking confirmation details and e-ticket to your registered email address and cellphone number after making a successful payment. You must have your e-ticket while boarding the bus.

Transitara advises to book your tickets in advance to secure the lowest ticket prices and reserve your bus seat for a trouble-free trip.

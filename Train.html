IRCTC Valid ID Proof for Train Travel

You don't need to enter ID details while booking train tickets on the app. However, passengers must carry a valid ID proof during their journey. The Travelling Ticket Examiner (TTE) will check your ID while verifying your ticket in the coach and may assist with seat allocation. Make sure to carry the original version of any of the following ID proofs:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Voter ID
  • Passport
  • Driving License
  • PAN Card
  • Photo ID issued by Central/State Government
  • Student ID with photograph
  • Nationalized Bank Passbook with photograph
  • Credit Card with laminated photograph
  • Any other photo ID card with a serial number
